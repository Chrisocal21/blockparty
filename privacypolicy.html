Privacy Policy

South O's Block Party Photos respects your privacy. This app only requests access to your Google Drive to upload photos and videos you select. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information or files. All uploads go directly to the event organizer's Google Drive and are not accessible to the public.

What information do we collect?

  • We do not collect or store any personal data.
  • We do not track, analyze, or share your usage.
  • Files you upload are sent directly to the event organizer's Google Drive and are not stored elsewhere.

How do we use your information?

  • We use your Google authentication only to allow you to upload files to the event organizer's Drive folder.
  • We do not use your information for any other purpose.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the event organizer.
